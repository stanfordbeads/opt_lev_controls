Checklist for Leaving:


ALWAYS CHECK

1) Hot-plate and oven

2) Roughing line pressure

3) Mako off to prevent computer crashes

4) Piezo dropper HV/RF amp off



WITH BEAD IN TRAP

1) Check laser state

2) Check valve state

3) Turn electrodes to 0 V

4) Pull cantilever all the way back (z = 0 um)



WITHOUT BEAD IN TRAP

1) Lasers off (?)

2) Leave chamber pumping, but turbo off

3) Cantilever back, bead dropper back
